Nopryl of Norway

"Never underestimate the power of stupid people in large groups!"

NoPryl of Norway is a gaming community, established in 2001.
Our raison d'être is organized coop-playing of the Arma series, although we are known to dabble in other games and activities as well.
The Arma department of NoPryl is known as the "Land/Air Make it go Boom Squad" or LAMBS for short.
In order to play with us, come say hi on our Discord server. We'll help you get set up.
